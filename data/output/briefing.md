# Daily Financial Briefing — April 05, 2026

**Daily Briefing – April 05, 2026**

**AAPL**
Apple’s stock closed at **$255.92**. Jim Cramer highlighted the company’s new CFO settling into the role, reaffirming his long-term bullish stance on the stock.
